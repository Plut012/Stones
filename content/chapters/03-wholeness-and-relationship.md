chapter: 3
title: "Wholeness and Relationship"
quote: "Understanding the relationship between each stone and the overall position."
---

No stone exists alone.

Each placement shifts the whole. Each stone speaks to all others — across distance, across time, across the entire board.

To see one stone is to see nothing.

To see the board is to see one stone's true power.

> Can you hold the whole and the part at once?
