---
chapter: 11
title: "The Journey Is the Destination"
quote: "Victory and defeat are both teachers. Only the practice remains."
---

Victory fades. Defeat fades.

Both teach. Both pass. Both are illusion.

The practice remains. Eternal. Always beginning. Never finished.

Play to be here. Play to understand. Play to express what cannot be spoken.

> When the game ends, what have you been doing?
