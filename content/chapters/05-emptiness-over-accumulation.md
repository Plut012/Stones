---
chapter: 5
title: "Emptiness Over Accumulation"
quote: "Like a cup that must be empty to be filled, the mind must be clear to see the board's truth."
---

The full cup cannot receive.

The cluttered mind cannot see.

Wisdom is not accumulation. Not knowledge piled upon knowledge.

Empty the cup. Clear the mind. Only then does the board speak its truth.

> What must you let go to see clearly?
