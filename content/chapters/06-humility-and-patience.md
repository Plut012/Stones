---
chapter: 6
title: "Humility and Patience"
quote: "The ego seeks victory. The spirit seeks understanding."
---

The ego demands. The spirit listens.

Patience is not waiting. It is being present without grasping.

Humility is not weakness. It is the courage to not know, to learn, to see.

Each stone asks: Will you let go of winning?

> What remains when victory no longer matters?
